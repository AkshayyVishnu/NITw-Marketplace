About Us

NITW Marketplace was founded in 2024 with a simple mission: to create a sustainable and efficient platform for NIT Warangal students to exchange goods within the campus community.

Our platform helps reduce waste, save money, and build a stronger campus community by facilitating peer-to-peer transactions for textbooks, electronics, furniture, and more.

Created by students, for students, we understand the unique needs of college life and strive to make exchanging items as seamless as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

+

Simply click on the "Signup" link in the navigation menu and fill out the required information. You'll need to use your college email address for verification.

How does the auction system work?

+

Users can list items for auction with a starting bid and duration. Other users can place bids until the auction ends, and the highest bidder wins the item.

Is there a fee for using the platform?

+

No, NITW Marketplace is completely free for all NIT Warangal students. We believe in fostering a community of sharing and sustainability.

How can I ensure safe transactions?

+

We recommend meeting in public places on campus for exchanges, using our in-app chat for communication, and using our rating system to verify trusted users.

What items are prohibited on the platform?

+

Prohibited items include illegal goods, weapons, alcohol, tobacco products, prescription medications, and any items that violate college policies.
